The Pod Name CFB Podcast


You’ve found it. You may rest your weary head and open your tired ears, as you have officially stumbled upon

the greatest college football podcast ever assembled. If you pride yourself on being interested in all thing’s college

football – the brands, the mascots, the coaches, the drama, the playoff, the unrivaled environment– then this is

undoubtedly the show for you. Come for expert, sometimes amateur, analysis, and stay for the banter, fun-loving coverage of

our great sport, inventive and unique segments, and some mediocre (at best) comedy. The PodNameCFB is uploaded bi-weekly (Mondays and

Thursdays during the season) and is your exclusive one stop shop for all the news and analysis you’ve been searching for. Forget the

bland studio products that you must trudge your way through,forget the non-innovative hosts you’ve grown to tolerate, and forget the bias of

sports media. Sit back and let us guide you through the wonderful world of college football.


The PodNameCFB was originally founded under the conditions of being ran by a single host – William

Ferri – in 2021 and carried a less than appropriate name. Though this original template was carried for

a few episodes, he was soon joined by 2 childhood friends to help realize his passion project, Donovan Ventura, and

Troy Thibodeau. After the 2021 college football season, however, the show fell into a long-winded hiatus. Throughout

the silence, the crew worked to create something more professional and palatable to the general masses, which was

finally brought to life at the beginning of the 2023 season and hasn’t looked back since.
