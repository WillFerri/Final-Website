Podcast Episodes

The continuation of a long lost podcast covering college
football has returned with the same hosts as last time,
now under the name Pod Name CFB. Covering everything
around the country week in and week out, uploads on
Monday and Thursday

Pod Name CFB - Early Bowl Season/What the Hell is a Blue Raider: Episode 34

The guys are back to review the early bowl matchups that occurred over the weekend, Troy does
not in fact know the mascot of middle tennessee state, and loads more fun topics and banter.

Pod Name CFB - Regular Season Review Special: Episode 33

Is it filler? perhaps. The college football slate has once again disappeared and given way to an
episode where we honestly didn’t know what to discuss, so we put the season in review. Check in
for The Pod Name CFB Hosts Award show, the 3 best coaching moves thus far, and a look back to the
predictions we made at the beginning of the season.

Pod Name CFB - America’s Game/Toilet Bowl Time: Episode 32

God bless our troops. The pod name cfb hosts are back to discuss the armed forces matchup
that occurred over the weekend, plus a whole lot more. The portal is on fire, national awards
were released before the weekend, full review included, and it’s high time for some crappy bowl
matchups.

Pod Name CFB - Immaculate Grids and Subpar Lists: Episode 31

Well, the college football regular season slate has come and gone like it has every year.
One regular season game remaining, Americas game, but we needed something to talk about more
than that. This is a pretty odd episode, rankings of the 5 best games of the year, 5 bowl matchups
each host is most excited for, an immaculate grid...

Pod Name CFB - A Great Tide Washes the Seminoles Away: Episode 30

Poor, poor seminoles. A controversial weekend of college football comes to a close, and your
favorite guys are back to discuss it. Check in for a breakdown and discussion of the playoff/new
years six release, conference championship game reviews, winners and losers...
