About The Hosts

Will Ferri

Hey, I’m Will. I’m 20, currently attending college at the University of South Carolina,
and have zero hobbies due to the class load every semester. I’m from the lovely
town of Indian Land, South Carolina and since I was kid I’ve been obsessed with
football at the collegiate level. I created this podcast originally to be a solo act, but 1 is
the loneliest number you could ever do, and 2 is just as sad as 1, so I needed some
co-hosts to join me. Who better to fill the role than Troy and Donovan, my childhood best
friends. Together we make up the greatest show ever conceived - The Pod Name CFB.
